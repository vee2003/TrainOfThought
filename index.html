Left Behind
Written By: Vanrose

Left Behind

Written By: Vanrose

Alone and scared, the little girl stands
With no one to hold her, no loving hands
She looks around, but no one's there
Her parents left her, it isn't fair

She cries and screams, but no one hears
Her heart is breaking, filled with fears
The night is cold, the wind is cruel
The little girl is just a lonely fool

She wonders why they left her here
Did they even love her, was she ever dear?
Her mind races, but finds no answer
Her parents are gone, like a dark disaster

The little girl is all alone
No family, no place to call her own
She hopes and prays, for a new start
To find someone to heal her broken heart

But for now, she stands alone
The little girl with no home
Abandoned, but still alive
Hoping someday, she'll thrive.

Middle Child
Written By: Vanrose

Middle Child

Written By: Vanrose

I am the forgotten one,
The one you never see.
Lost in the middle of it all,
A stranger to your company.

I am the one you never hear,
Lost in the crowd of voices.
My secrets locked away inside,
A victim of my own choices.

I am the one you never touch,
A shadow in the light.
My body is my prison now,
A place where wrong and right collide.

I am the one who cries alone,
The one who begs for help.
But no one sees or hears my pain,
No one believes in what I've felt.

So here I am, the middle child,
Forgotten and alone.
But still, I'll find my way out,
And make my presence known.

Suffering in Silence
Written By: Vanrose

Suffering in Silence

Written By: Vanrose

I am the middle child, forgotten and ignored
Lost in the shuffle, a faceless name on a board
But I have a story, one that's hard to tell
Of secrets and pain, and a living hell

I trusted someone, a mistake I now see
He took advantage, and put his hands on me
But when I spoke up, no one believed
The pain and the hurt, I was forced to receive

I cry in the night, but no one hears my call
The pain is so real, but invisible to all
I suffer in silence, the weight of it all
On my shoulders, I bear this heavy burden

I long to be seen, to be heard and believed
To be held in someone's arms, and to be relieved
But for now, I suffer in silence, a forgotten child
Alone with my pain, my innocence defiled.

A Place to Belong
Written By: Vanrose

A Place to Belong

Written By: Vanrose

I was lonely, in this world so vast,
Longing for company that would last,
Wandering through life without a friend,
Hoping for someone who would lend
An ear to my thoughts, a heart to my fears,
Someone who'd laugh with me, and wipe away my tears,
But the days went on, and I was alone,
Lost in a sea of faces unknown.

Until one day, I met a kind soul,
Who made me feel like I was whole,
They welcomed me in, and showed me their care,
And suddenly, the world seemed less unfair.

We laughed and we talked, and we shared our dreams,
And slowly but surely, things weren't as they seem,
For I found a place where I felt I belonged,
A place where my heart could finally sing its song.

And now, I am grateful for the friends I've found,
For they picked me up when I was on the ground,
They showed me love, and they showed me light,
And for that, I will hold them close with all my might.

So if you're feeling lonely, and lost in the crowd,
Remember that somewhere, your friends can be found,
And when you do, hold on tight,
For they will make everything feel just right.

Betrayed Trust
Written By: Vanrose

Betrayed Trust

Written By: Vanrose

Betrayed by those I loved and held dear,
Left with a heart so full of fear,
Friends who were once so close to me,
And a lover I trusted, so foolishly.

Their lies and deceit, cut me like a knife,
Leaving me with a shattered life,
A heart that's broken, and trust undone,
A sense of betrayal that's second to none.

I find it hard to trust, to let someone in,
To believe that their love isn't a temporary whim,
For I've learned the hard way, that even the best,
Can turn out to be just like the rest.

The wounds still hurt, and the scars still show,
A reminder of a pain that I'll never outgrow,
And yet, I know that I must try,
To move on, to let go, to spread my wings and fly.

For in this world, there are those who are true,
Who will stick by you no matter what you do,
Who will love you and support you through thick and thin,
And help you find your way to happiness again.

So though my heart may be wounded and sore,
I know that I must try once more,
To trust again, to open my heart,
And let love be a brand new start.

Trust for Love
Written By: Vanrose

Trust for Love

Written By: Vanrose

I've been hurt before, I've been betrayed,
My heart's been shattered, my trust's been swayed.
And now, I'm scared to let someone in,
To open up and love again.

But there's this guy who's caught my eye,
Who makes me feel like I can fly.
He's kind and gentle, sweet and true,
And I can't help falling for him, it's what my heart wants to do.

Yes, there's an age gap between us, that's true,
But our love is real, and that's all that matters too.
I know people might judge, they might stare,
But I'm willing to take the risk, to show them that love doesn't care.

I'm scared, yes, but I'm willing to try,
To take a leap of faith, to reach for the sky.
I'm willing to risk it all for this guy,
To trust in him, and give our love a try.

So even though my heart is still healing,
And my fears make my mind keep reeling,
I know that I can't give up on love,
For my heart yearns for love's sweet embrace,
And I cannot let fear take its place,

I'll tread lightly but with an open heart,
And trust that love will do its part.
For in this risk I find my hope,
And with this man, I'll learn to cope.
